My Poems

Ice Princess

She walks in silence, with a calm demeanour.
She wears ice like a gown,
But with a smile like a crack in the glacier.
Her eyes, crystal-sharp—her breath, like a blizzard.

Her beauty is elegant, untouchable, piercing.
It pierces my heart
And makes my love bleed
Through every drop of blood.

I wish I could slip on the ice and fall in the crack,
Reaching your heart and the love inside.
Never ever going outside,
Just lost there in peace.
But… but would it ever happen outside my dream?

Maybe in another winter, I’ll find spring in you;
Outside the dream… eventually.
Or… will I?

The Wisest Fool

If my life is a dark room,
then you are the only candle burning here,
illuminating my heart with your flame.

If my life is a ship,
then you are the only anchor holding me.

If my life is a desert,
then you are the only oasis I’m chasing.

When the candle will be extinguished,
When the anchor will break from rust,
When the oasis will fade into a mirage,

I will fade into the darkness.
I will be lost in the sea.
I will be scattered into the sands of the desert.

You sculpted my soul.
You created tides in my veins.
You set a fire in my heart’s forest.
I became the wisest fool ever lived.

You may never look back,
But I shall keep walking your way.
And if you ever did,
You would see the crater you created unknowingly.
And the fire burning in it.

Delusional

I know it's a delusion
But I don't want to leave it
I know it won't happen
But I can't admit it
I sip lies like fine wine
Drinking poison that turns me blind
It burns my throat
While the truth is choked

Dreams made from shattered glass
More broken than ruins of a building that
long ago collapsed
Walking paths that never existed
Building things that are already broken
Lighting a fire from ashes
Chasing hopes that are never meant to be met

Refusing to admit my hopes are fake
Grieving for something
That died before it could have lived
